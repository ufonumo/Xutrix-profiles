About us.

We are a creative Architecture Studio

Xutrix's unique collaboration between in-house architects, engineers and experienced technical staff allows us to anticipate problems before they occur. Our service is direct, hands-on and highly responsive. We create tailored solutions to complex problems. We’re smart about buildings.

Here, our services range from developing New Construction Projects to Restoring Existing buildings. This is made possible through Design Development, Bid Services and Value Engineering, Progress Monitoring, Investigation and Repairs, Architectural Design, etc.

Our Mission

We want to augument the existing values for products and services to the world through adoption of creative skills and technological innovation in product and services delivery. The products and services delivered by this company are more oriented to user health and well-being as well as overall satisfaction from use and reuse.
